About my research

My research interests are broadly in low-dimensional topology. More specifically, I explore the existence and uniqueness of surfaces in the 4-ball bounding a given knot. Most of the time, I study these surfaces by examining their induced maps on Khovanov homology. As a result, I have spent a significant amount of time trying to understand the definitions/properties of these induced maps!
I would like to learn more about:
  • 4-manifolds
  • Heegaard-Floer homology
  • knot concordance
  • knotted surfaces in 4-manifolds
  • anything you're working on!

My research interests are in low-dimensional topology. I spend most of my time thinking about induced maps on Khovanov homology and their applications toward 3 and 4-dimensional properties of surfaces bounding links (e.g., sliceness of knots, uniqueness of surfaces bounding a fixed knot, etc).
I would like to learn more about:
  • 4-manifolds
  • Heegaard-Floer homology
  • knot concordance
  • knotted surfaces in 4-manifolds
  • anything you're working on!
Papers:
  • Khovanov homology and exotic surfaces in the 4-ball, joint with Kyle Hayden, in preparation
  • Distinguishing slice disks using Khovanov homology, in preparation
  • Relative Khovanov-Jacobsson classes, joint with Jonah Swann, submitted
  • Multiplication operators on weighted Banach spaces of a tree, joint with Robert F. Allen, Bulletin of the Korean Mathematical Society

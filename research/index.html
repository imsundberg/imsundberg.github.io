My research interests are in low-dimensional topology. I have spent time working with 3-manifolds, specifically Stallings' Fibering Theorem ('62), the topic of my master's thesis. My current interests are more directed toward 4-manifolds, specifically, slice & shake genus, knot concordance, and the Slice-Ribbon Conjecture.

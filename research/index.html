My research interests are in low-dimensional topology. I've spent most of my graduate career thinking about induced maps on Khovanov homology and their applications toward boundary-preserving isotopy classes of surfaces in the 4-ball (e.g. uniqueness of slice disks for a fixed knot).
I would like to learn more about:
  • 4-manifolds
  • Heegaard-Floer homology
  • knot concordance
  • knotted surfaces in 4-manifolds
Publications:
  • Relative Khovanov-Jacobsson classes, joint with Jonah Swann
  • Multiplication operators on weighted Banach spaces of a tree, joint with Robert F. Allen, Bulletin of the Korean Mathematical Society

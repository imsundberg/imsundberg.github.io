Professional Development Seminar

The Professional Development Seminar is a monthly meeting of students in the Graduate Group in Science and Mathematics which offers insights and advice about various aspects of professional practice. Over lunch, guest speakers facilitate discussions about professional development with graduate students and faculty.
I organized this seminar in 2018 and 2019.

About me

I am a software engineer at MSP (Mathematical Sciences Publishers). Previously, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics, a PhD student at Bryn Mawr College, and an undergraduate at the University of Wisconsin La Crosse (check out their Campus Connections article which talks about my journey from UWL to MPIM).
My research interests are in low-dimensional topology.

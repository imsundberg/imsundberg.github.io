About me

I am a mathematician currently working as a postoctoral fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics. I completed my PhD at Bryn Mawr College in 2022, and beforehand, I was a student at the University of Wisconsin La Crosse.
My research interests are in low-dimensional topology, where I use Khovanov homology theories to study knots and surfaces in 3- and 4-manifolds.
My pronouns are they/them.

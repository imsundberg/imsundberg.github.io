About me

My name is Isaac Sundberg; my pronouns are he/they.
I am a sixth-year graduate student in mathematics at Bryn Mawr College, and I expect to graduate this spring (2022). My advisor is Paul Melvin.
My research interests are in low-dimensional topology, and in particular, I use Khovanov homology to study 3 and 4-dimensional properties of surfaces bounding knots and links.

I enjoy math, but I also enjoy music, climbing, and playing chess.

Graduate Student Assosciation (2016-2020)

The Graduate Student Association (GSA) is a graduate student run organization at Bryn Mawr College with the purpose of disseminating relevant information and concerns between the college and graduate student body.
Together with Elena Gittleman (History of Art), I served as the co-chair of the GSA throughout 2017-2019.
Together with Audrey Wallace (Classics), I served on the college's Graduate Council. There, we advocated for the creation of a Parental Leave Policy to ensure that all graduate students anticipating the birth or adoption of a child are guaranteed continued funding and health care for an extended period of time. The policy we authored was implemented in 2018 and covers graduate students for a minimum of 8 weeks.
I served one final year on the GSA as the Mathematics Department Representative in 2019-2020.

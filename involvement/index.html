--- layout: default title: --- Graduate Student Association (2016-2020)
The Graduate Student Association (GSA) is a graduate student run organization at Bryn Mawr College with the purpose of disseminating relevant information and concerns between the college and graduate student body.
Together with Elena Gittleman, I served as the co-chair of the GSA throughout the academic years 2017-2019.
My most proud contribution to the college was the result of a collaboration with Audrey Wallace, with whom I served on the college's Graduate Council. Together, we advocated for the creation of a Parental Leave Policy to ensure that all graduate students anticipating the birth or adoption of a child are guaranteed continued funding and health care for an extended period of time. The inclusive policy we authored was implemented in 2018 and covers graduate students for a minimum of 8 weeks.
I served one final year on the GSA as the Department Representative from math.
Graduate Research Seminar (2019)
The Graduate Research Seminar is a biweekly seminar at Bryn Mawr College that is organized by graduate students, for graduate students.
I co-founded the seminar with my peer Lindsay Dever in 2019 with the purpose of giving graduate students the opportunity to present research in a comfortable, unsupervised environment. We are funded by Bryn Mawr's graduate student chapter of the American Mathematical Society.
I organized the seminar in 2019.
Professional Development Seminar (2018-2019)
The Professional Development Seminar is a monthly seminar at Bryn Mawr College that provides graduate students the opportunity to learn about professional development techniques by bringing in guest speakers from across Bryn Mawr's campus.
I organized the seminar over the academic year 2018-2019.
Voluntary Webpage Design (2015-2020)
One of my secret interests is webpage design, and throughout my academic career, I have voluntarily edited and created web-based content for the organizations in which I am involved. This includes the above organizations along, along with many others.
